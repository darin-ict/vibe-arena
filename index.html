Terms of Battle

1. Fair Play: Competitors must use the integrated AI models provided by the platform. Any external automation or code injection will result in immediate disqualification and forfeiture of stakes.

2. Stakes & Payouts: All matches require a $12.50 entry fee. Payouts ($20) are processed via Solana within 10 minutes of match conclusion.

3. Judging: All decisions made by the Gemini AI Judge are final. No appeals will be considered unless a technical system failure occurs.

4. Age Requirement: You must be 18+ to participate in real-money battles.

Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection: We collect only essential information: your email for access and your wallet address for payouts.

2. Code Ownership: Code generated during battles remains private to the participants and is used only by the AI judge for evaluation.

3. Security: We do not store your private keys. All transactions are handled through secure web3 providers.

4. Third Parties: Your email will never be sold. It is used exclusively for platform notifications.
